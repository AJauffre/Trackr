Install

With a few simple tools your Bike Tracker is easy to install. Connect the Bike Tracker to your battery, fit it in a discreet location of your choosing and you’re ready to go. You now have world wide 24/7 access to the location of your motorbike.

Ride

Once you have registered and installed your Bike Tracker you’re ready to ride. Activate your Bike Tracker with our mobile app when you park or use the scheduling tool to activate when its most convenient for you.
