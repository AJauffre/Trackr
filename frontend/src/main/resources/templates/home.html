What is Bike-Tracker?

Bike-Tracker is an anti-theft device that integrates GPS, motion sensor, and silent alarm technology to monitor your motorcycle. Bike-Tracker leverages a worldwide satellite network to provide global monitoring and alerting to keep you connected.

How it Works

Register

When you receive your Bike-Tracker you'll need to register on our secure online portal. It will only take five minutes and the process is explained 'here'. Once registered, your bike tracker is ready to install.

Install

With a few simple tools and our installation guide you’ll be taken through the process of fitting your Bike-Tracker. We’ll supply you with a few fitting options, but choose a location which is best for you.

Ready to Ride

When your Bike-Tracker is installed you're ready to ride. Arm your Bike-Tracker's motion sensor and silent alarm using the Bike-Tracker mobile application or secure portal and rest easy knowing your motorcycle is safe.

Features
Worldwide Cover

Your Bike-Tracker provides the same features no matter where you are in the world - whether you commute to work or travel around the world you'll be able to track the location of your motorcycle.

GPS Tracking

Satellites track the location of your Bike-Tracker at all times. In the event of theft, you can provide the authorities temporary access to your GPS data allowing them to recover your motorcycle.

Motion Sensor

The motion sensor contained in your Bike-Tracker is always ready. It will activate your silent alarm at the first sign of any continual movement of your motorcycle when you're not around.

Silent Alarm

If Bike-Tracker's motion sensor recognizes unauthorised movement the silent alarm activates. You’ll be notified by push notification, SMS, telephone, or e-mail. Use the options that are best for you.

Notifications

Bike-Tracker gives you four ways to be notified. You will have access to an unlimited number of alerts so there's no need to worry about additional purchases!

IP67 Rated

Bike-Tracker is IP67 rated, meaning that it is robust enough to be impervious from dust and water resistant. Bike-Tracker is designed for vehicles so it's also shock resistant.
